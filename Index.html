Our  Love  Story

It All Started When We Met Online

Ottep and Kate met for the first time in February 2020, and they just clicked. Then COVID happened, but even with everything slowing down, they kept talking and continued seeing each other.

By September 2020, Ottep asked Kate to be his girlfriend in Tagaytay with Taal right in front of them. That was where everything really started, and from then on, they spent most of their time together, from random food trips to slow, lazy weekends and fun adventures.

Over time, they became not just partners but best friends, sharing the same humor, little habits, and plans for the future.

Last September 2025, Ottep proposed in the mountains of Siquijor, just a few days before their fifth anniversary. It was their first time there, and it felt right being somewhere surrounded by nature, something they've always loved. And now, on February 6, 2027, they're getting married and excited to start the next chapter together.
