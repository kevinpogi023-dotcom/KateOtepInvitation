Our  Love  Story

It All Started When We
Both Met Online

Ottep and Kate first met last February 2020, and they instantly clicked. Soon after, the world slowed down because of COVID, but that didn't stop them from getting to know each other. They kept talking, continued seeing each other, and their connection only grew stronger.

By September 2020, Ottep asked Kate to be his girlfriend in Tagaytay with Taal right in front of them. That was where their story truly began. Since then, they've enjoyed countless food trips, slow weekends, and adventures together.

They just naturally clicked. Over time, they became not just partners but best friends, sharing the same humor, little habits, and plans for the future.

Last September 2025, Ottep proposed at the top of a mountain in Siquijor during their anniversary trip. It was their first time there, and being surrounded by nature made the moment even more meaningful. Now, on February 6, 2027, they're excited to begin their next chapter together as husband and wife.
